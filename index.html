📃 Rules:

Each round a player can either roll dice or hold.

🎲 Roll dice:

If your roll is above 1, it gets added to your current score.

Roll a 1 – and you will lose your current score. And your turn.

Pretty devastating, huh? That is why you can also

📥 Hold:

When you hold, your current score is added to the total score.

You still lose your turn, but hey. You can't have everything in life.

But how do I... You know, 🏆 win?

The first player to reach a total score of 100 or above wins!

It's that simple!
